Background of the study



The children of PPHC are kids that came from broken families, their homes are not capable of upbringing a child either because of the absence of their parents due to being active in illegal actions and being in jail or their neglect of their kids, therefore their basic necessities cannot be fulfilled. As a result, the nuns of PPHC decided to take in some of these children because of the horrible living conditions and to provide them with their basic needs such as food, shelter, and education. Although they are able to meet these, there are still some setbacks, the biggest being their lack of education and education materials.

Problem

Children from PPHC are neglected and abandoned, therefore they are malnourished and academically challenged. Malnourishment can cause the brain to underdevelop; the child’s speech, coordination, and memory may all be affected by nutritional deficits, which may hasten the rate at which your brain loses neurons.
